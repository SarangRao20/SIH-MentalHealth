About Mental Health Assessments
Why Take These Assessments?

These standardized tools help identify symptoms and track your mental health over time. They are used by healthcare professionals worldwide and provide valuable insights into your wellbeing.

Important Notes:
  • These assessments are not a substitute for professional diagnosis
  • Results are confidential and encrypted for your privacy
  • Regular monitoring can help track progress and identify concerning patterns
  • Consider sharing results with a mental health professional
Remember: If you're experiencing thoughts of self-harm or suicide, please seek immediate help by calling 988 or visiting your nearest emergency room.
